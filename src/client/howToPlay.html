    Tools

    Trying to... do things? Maybe try using a:

    • Poke! Left click to grab and move things, right click to use them!
    • Bow! Left click to shoot! Arrows can break blocks during combat.
    • Pick! Left click to instantly destroy blocks, right click to deliver a devastating blow!
    • Brick! Left click to place blocks, right click to remove them!
    • Arrow! Hold left click to fly!
